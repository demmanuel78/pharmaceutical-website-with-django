Company Information

How We Started

Daitech pharmaceuticals was incorporated in Nigeria in December 2014. Registration formalities with NAFDAC for our products commenced in early 2015, and this was concluded for most of the products we are currently marketing in 2015 and ongoing for the new ones from different OEMs. Full commercialization of business commenced in 2015.

We Are Trusted Company

We are a fast-growing Nigerian pharmaceutical focused on the manufacturing, sourcing, importing and marketing our own brands of various ranges of pharmaceutical products nationwide, which we commercialize and distribute through our marketing infrastructure in tune with the needs of our customers.

The company distributes its products through sales representatives, wholesalers, retail pharmacies, hospitals and a national network of distributors.
